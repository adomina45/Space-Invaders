Space Invaders

Final Project created for CSCI343, Computer Graphics, at the College of the Holy Cross with Juan Correa & Justin Bella using Javascript and WebGL as well as libraries supplied by our courses textbook "Interactive Computer Graphics: A top-down approach with WebGL, 7th Edition, by Edward Angel & Dave Shreiner, Addison Wesley".

Oops ... your browser doesn't support the HTML5 canvas element

Controls

"S" - starts game

"Z" - pauses game

"J" - go left

"K" - fire

"L" - go right

"1" - rotate up (when not in screen view)

"2" - rotate down (when not in screen view)

"3" - rotate left (when not in screen view)

"4" - rotate right (when not in screen view)
